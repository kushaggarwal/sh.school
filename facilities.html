ADDED FACILITIES

Apart from these facilities, SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu also has a Big Skating Rink, a Volley Ball Court and Badminton Court in the school premises. In addition to that, all the classrooms in the school are well lit and air conditioned. The digital initiative of Educomp Smart Class has been well incorporated into our education system for students to have comprehensive learning. The last but of course not the least we have introduced the electronic system of attendance for our children as their security is our biggest responsibility. The system works in a manner wherein the special I-card of the student is scanned the moment he/she enters the school and simultaneously parents are informed about their arrival at school. Same is repeated at the time of dispersal as well.
