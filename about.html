About Us

Since 1989, India’s first playschool chain has worked towards providing quality education, through a vibrant and child-friendly system of learning. The award winning school chain has laid the foundation of success for more than 3,50,000 children across India, Nepal and Bangladesh. The chain is remarkably managed and driven by the team of renowned educationists, the Arora clan, which has revolutionised education. After making a landmark in the field of ‘Preschool Education’, SHEMROCK has worked towards providing same stress free learning to the 10+2 segment. SHEMFORD Futuristic School, a milestone in its journey, is a senior secondary school empowering children with essential skills required to explore new opportunities on global level. SHEMFORD Futuristic Schools are one of the first schools in India to become the fastest growing school chain all over India and have, thus, bagged the award for Limca Book of Records in 2013.

SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu, the unit of SHEMROCK & SHEMFORD Group of Schools is located at Birpur near Imperial Resort, Samba in the beautiful state of Jammu & Kashmir. Equipped with an innovative curriculum, SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu offers a child-friendly environment with effective modern teaching methods. The stress free learning helps prepare the children to be responsible, self-confident, disciplined and strong individuals. The colourful, chirpy and inspiring environment allows children sufficient space and time to stimulate learning which helps children grow actively in a natural way.

We, at SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu use a unique system of education & curriculum – ShemEduMAXTM, which helps children gain school readiness in all areas of academics and social learning. Our specially designed in-house curriculum incorporates the latest research and practices by keeping in view the needs of each and every learner into account. ShemEduMAXTM is a complete educational system that helps every child, at early stages to become a part of a quality learning environment to not only become creative but also confident & brilliant thinker. It offers a learning environment which is conducive for children with an innovative curriculum, engaging teaching methods and personalised assessment that helps every child grow intellectually and succeed. We create opportunities to learn basic skills, such as self-dependency, critical thinking and decision making.

At SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu world class education is provided to our students, which helps them to develop their abilities for a brighter future. SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu has varying options to offer from Playgroup to Class VIII. The world class curriculum and enriching life skills help prepare our budding stars for the future. We believe in teaching our children Honesty, Discipline, Truthfulness, Responsibility and Respect for others. We focus on the child’s ability to express the feelings which helps develop self-confidence. We are surely going to set up a benchmark in the field of education with our methodologies.

Mission

SHEMFORD Futuristic Schools progress with the mission ‘To make learning creative, interesting, interactive and engaging, through a system which is constructive, comprehensive, practical and futuristic.’ Therefore, we offer a creative, caring, innovative, stress-free and harmonious learning environment for children by imparting quality education through hands-on material and other useful aids.
