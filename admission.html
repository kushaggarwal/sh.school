Admission

Education at SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu focuses on the development of skills, talents and aptitudes of its students. The school offers learning opportunities from Playgroup to Class VIII, with a multitude of facilities to offer and an assortment of amenities to enhance life learning skills in our SHEMFORD Stars.

Our endeavor is to deliver world class education to our students by guiding them under the CBSE Pattern. If you are looking for admission of your child in the state of Jammu & Kashmir, SHEMFORD Futuristic School, Jammu is the right choice.
